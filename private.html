FULL & PARTIAL RESTAURANTS EVENTS

We welcome groups to exclusively reserve Golden Kitchen and treat their guest to our exciting approach to modern bistronomy. We can accommodate standing receptions of up to 115 guests or seated dinners of up to 70 in main room.



LARGE GROUP DINNER EVENTS

We can also accommodate dinner groups with a specially curated menu in the dining room. Groups will be seated in our dinning room at either one table or multiple adjacent tables. A variety of dinning options are available from a family-style communal feast to a seasonallay inspired tasting menu. Please contact us for more information. We look forward to hosting you.
